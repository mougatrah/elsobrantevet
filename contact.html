Directions

From Highway 80

From Highway 80, take the Appian Way exit and go east for about 1 ½ miles ( you’ll pass the Post Office on the right) continue through the light at Appain Way / Valley View Road, take the first right on to Sobrante Avenue, then make an immediate right into the parking lot.

From Highway 24

Take the Orinda exit, then Camino Pablo toward Richmond/Orinda Village; continue on Camino Pablo/San Pablo Dam Road past the reservoir; you’ll make a right at Valley View Road, and continue to Sobrante Avenue; turn left at Sobrante Avenue, then make an immediate right into the parking lot.
